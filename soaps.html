What is felted soap?

Hand wrapped layers of merino wool around a bar of soap, it is an excellent natural exfoliant for hands and body, serving as both soap and washcloth. 
The wool is non-slippery and water repellent, drying fast between uses without leaving a soapy residue, and has a lifespan 2-3 times longer than a typical bar of soap, with the natural wool's antimicrobial properties maintaining a hygienic state, never becoming moldy.
With each wash, the wool conforms around the bar of soap, ensuring a tightly fitted felt layer until you are left with an ornate scrubber, perfect for use during cleaning.

Each design is one of a kind, making it the perfect long lasting gift for Christmas stocking stuffers, Valentine’s Day, Mother’s Day, a Birthday, or any day for somebody special!
